Caramelised Onions

Description

Soft, richly flavoured caramelised onions are a wonderful accompaniment to cold meats, pates and terrines, and make a great base for savoury tarts. They're very easy to make at home - you simply cook the onions slowly to caramelise their natural sugars, while the brown sugar and balsamic help the process along.

Ingredients

  • White onions
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Heat about 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium-low heat.
  2. Once hot, add the onions and stir to coat.
  3. Add sugar ater 15-20 minutes of cooking.
  4. Keep stirring every 5-10 minutes, until deeply caramelized, about 1 Â½ - 2 hours.
  5. If the onions start to burn at any point, lower the heat slightly and add a splash of water to deglaze any bits stuck to the pan.
  6. Season with salt and set aside to cool completely.
