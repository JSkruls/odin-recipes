Eggs Royale

Description

Eggs Royale is a delightful breakfast or brunch! Lightly toasted English muffins topped with smoked salmon, soft poached eggs, and a rich and creamy Hollandaise sauce.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • White wine vinegar
  • Hollandaise sauce
  • Slice of sourdough
  • Smoked salmon
  • Horseradish sauce
  • Baby salad leaves
  • Sliced shallot
  • Sliced radishes
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Bring a deep saucepan of water to a boil. Add the vinegar. Stiring the water crack the egg into the water and carefully tip it into the centre of the saucepan.
  2. Cook for 3-4 minutes. Lift the egg out and drain it on a piece of kitchen paper.
  3. Warm up the hollandaise sauce in a small saucepan.
  4. Toast the sourdough. Spread the horseradish sauce over and arrange the salmon on top.
  5. Put the poached egg on the middle of the toast and pour the hollandaise over.
  6. Garnish with the salad leaves, shallot and radish slices, cress and a drizzle of the olive oil.
